About Bigfoot

I'm out of the forest and into the spotlight. I've been calling the Pacific Northwest home for a while now and I'm looking to freshen up the place. I'm here to shun all the non-believers and really freshen up the place, and that's why I love Bigfoot Garden. All of the plants may look the same but they're all very special to me.
